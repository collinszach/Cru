iOS 26 Liquid Glass
Apple's design language for 2026. Translucent materials, real-time blur, organic layering. This is what iOS developers build to right now — and it perfectly fits a wine app's warmth.
Background
Warm cream gradient with a subtle garnet blush at the top — like light through a wine glass. Not white, not dark. Feels alive.
Cards
Frosted glass with backdrop blur. Each card is translucent — the warm background bleeds through. Elevated cards get more opacity and stronger shadow.
Typography
SF Pro Display for vintage years — ultra-thin weight, enormous, in garnet. System font — looks native and reads perfectly at any size. No custom fonts needed for premium.
Tab Bar
Liquid glass tab bar — transparent blur over whatever's beneath it. Garnet scanner button floats above with a white ring halo. Exactly what iOS 26 tab bars look like natively.
Drinking Window
Small dot, top-right of every card. Green = peak now. Gold = approaching. Muted = hold. Glanceable without cluttering the card.
